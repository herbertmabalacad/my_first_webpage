Here at MathEase, we make math easier by teaching you how to solve problems using MDAS — that means...


Multiply
Multiply means repeated addition!

Whenever you see multiplication, solve it before anything else.
Example: 3 + 4 × 2
Start with 4 × 2 = 8
Now the problem becomes:
3 + 8 = 11

Divide
Divide is sharing or grouping equally!

Do division right after multiplication.
Example: 12 ÷ 3 + 2
Start with 12 ÷ 3 = 4
Now the problem becomes:
4 + 2 = 6

Add
Add means putting numbers together!

Addition comes after all multiplication and division are done.
Example: 4 + 3 = 7

Subtract
Subtract means taking away!

Subtraction is the last step.
Example: 10 - 2 = 8
